? Antidote.?

An antidote is a substance which can counteract a form of poisoning. While usually a word reserved for a poisonous snake or spider bites New Brighton's Antidote was created as a kind of anti-venom to the world at large.

During the industrialization era in the 19th century, food manufacturing arose. With this so did the use of fertilizers, pesticides, genetic modification and preservation agents. While exstensive tests by various government departments around the world supposedly cleared these new foods as fit for consumption.

There is a growing body of evidence which suggests that in fact these highly processed foods are responsible for producing many of the illnesses experienced in our modern societies. Work from the like of Max Gerson suggest that diet has a massive impact on our state of health. In the opinion of this author his studies conclusively prove that by eating a diet of organically grown fruit and vegetables can also be a cure a cancer.

As the great Socrates said 'let food be thy medicine'. Antidote aims to embody this simple wisdom by serving patrons with a variety of of organic salads, juices and waters.

Antidote is also a meditative atmosphere where can you practice yoga, mediate, enjoy Reki or a massage.
